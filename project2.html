Introduction

The Petrol Scam Detector is designed to prevent fraudulent activities at petrol stations by accurately measuring the amount of petrol dispensed. This system ensures that customers receive the exact amount of petrol they pay for, thereby preventing scams and ensuring transparency.

Working Principle

The system operates using a fluid flow meter to measure the amount of petrol dispensed. The flow meter sends signals to the Arduino UNO, which processes the data and displays the amount of petrol dispensed on an LCD display with an I2C module. The key components and steps involved are:

  • Fluid Flow Meter: Measures the flow rate of petrol and sends signals to the Arduino UNO.
  • Arduino UNO: Processes the signals from the flow meter and calculates the amount of petrol dispensed.
  • LCD Display with I2C Module: Displays the amount of petrol dispensed in real-time.

The system operates as follows:

  1. The fluid flow meter measures the flow rate of petrol as it is dispensed.
  2. The flow meter sends signals to the Arduino UNO, which processes the data and calculates the amount of petrol dispensed.
  3. The calculated amount is displayed on the LCD display in real-time, allowing customers to verify the amount of petrol they receive.

Components Used

  • Fluid Flow Meter: Measures the flow rate of petrol.
  • Arduino UNO: Microcontroller used to process the signals from the flow meter and calculate the amount of petrol dispensed.
  • LCD Display with I2C Module: Displays the amount of petrol dispensed in real-time.
  • Power Supply: Provides the necessary power to the components.
  • Connecting Wires: Used to connect the components together.

Applications

  • Petrol Stations: Ensures that customers receive the exact amount of petrol they pay for, preventing fraudulent activities.
  • Fuel Dispensers: Can be integrated into fuel dispensers to provide accurate measurements of fuel dispensed.
  • Quality Control: Ensures the accuracy and reliability of fuel dispensing systems.

Future Enhancements

  • IoT Integration: Integrate the system with IoT capabilities to provide real-time data to a central monitoring system, allowing for remote monitoring and control.
  • Mobile App Development: Develop a mobile app for real-time monitoring and control of the system, providing alerts and notifications to users.
  • Enhanced Sensor Accuracy: Use advanced sensors with higher accuracy and sensitivity for improved detection capabilities.
  • Battery Backup: Add a battery backup to ensure the system remains operational during power outages.
